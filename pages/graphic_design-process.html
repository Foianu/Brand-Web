Creative Brief

A creative brief is a document that’s aimed to help the designer understand the scope of the project and what’s needed from them.

Brief should include: company details, guidelines, target audience, define the problem, final product expectations, budget, viewers expectations.

Conduct Research

The research phase involves: looking at competitor designs, look at what your target audience is engaging in, look for trends, applying the knowledge.

Ideas

Brainstorming and ideation phase represent exploring ideas and making a few sketches.

Presenting sketches to the client in order to implement the project.

Create

Designing and adding details on approved sketches.

Refine

Polishing and refining the final design
